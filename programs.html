Web Design and Technologies

Web design is a process of conceptualizing, planning, and building a collection of electronic files that determine the layout, colors, text styles, structure, graphics, images, and use of interactive features that deliver pages to your site visitors.

  • Bronx Community College | Web Design
  • Borough of Manhattan Community College | Multimedia Programming

Industrial Design

Industrial design is a process of design applied to products that are to be manufactured through techniques of mass production.

  • City Tech | Industrial Design
  • LaGuardia Community College | Industrial Design

Computer Graphics and Animation

Computer animation is the art of creating moving images via the use of computers. It is a subfield of computer graphics and animation. Increasingly it is created by means of 3D computer graphics, though 2D computer graphics are still widely used for low bandwidth and faster real-time rendering needs.

  • Hostos Community College | Digital Design and Animation
  • Lehman College | Computer Graphics and Imaging
  • Borough of Manhattan Community College | Animation and Motion Graphics
  • City Tech | Communication Design
  • City Tech | Communication Design Management
  • York College | Communications Technology

Digital and Graphic Design

Digital Artist is an artist first, a communicator second. In professional terms, a Graphic Designer is a visual problem solver; their job is to creatively solve various communication challenges via the combination of typography, graphics, imagery, and in some fields, sound (e.g. motion and web).

  • Bronx Community College | Digital Arts: Graphic Design
  • Baruch College | Graphic Communications
  • Borough of Manhattan Community College | Multimedia Arts and Design
  • City College | Electronic Design and Multimedia
  • Kingsborough College | Graphic Design and Illustration
  • LaGuardia Community College | New Media Technology
  • Queens College | Graphic Design
  • Queensborough Community College | Digital Arts and Design

Game Design

Game design is a large field, drawing from the fields of computer science/programming, creative writing, and graphic design. Game designers take the creative lead in imagining and bringing to life video game worlds.

  • Hostos Community College | Game Design
