About this site.

Making the decisions to go to college or to come back may be one of the most difficult and important decision a person can make. It is, unfortunately, very stressful and may deem impossible to some. We strive on making your transition from High School to College or from AS to BA seamless. Instead to stifling through various webpages trying to keep track of who offers what and when, we collectively summarized all CUNY design programs in simple "get to the point" language.
Let us do the hard work!
